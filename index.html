About

CodeCraft is a discord community based on programming, coding and helping newbies on their journey to potential experts of their language. You want to learn this new super cool language that everyone is talking about, but don’t know if you can find people to help you? That’s our role! You scared people will make fun of you as you ask too “basic” questions? That’s our role to ensure that!

Rules

  • Please keep it clean.

  • Be respectful.

  • Never post any malicious links.

  • Push yourself to be your best self personally & skill wise.

  • Help others when and if you can.

  • Please do not use this server for commercial use.

  • Do not promote your services.

  • No illegal topics (e.g: hacking, security).
